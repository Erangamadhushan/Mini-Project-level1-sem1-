About Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa Beach Sri Lanka is the name of a small town which is located on the south coast of Sri Lanka around 98 KM on the south of Colombo. It is well known for the Hikkaduwa beach which is labeled as one of the best surfing site in Sri Lanka and for Coral Sanctuary which is located a few meters away from the shore.

Weather

Hikkaduwa Beach in Sri Lanka is a hub of adventure and relaxation. It's known for its exciting water sports and diverse food offerings. The beach also houses numerous spa centers for relaxation, comfortable accommodations with various amenities, and a range of tours for cultural and wildlife exploration.

Facilities

Despite tourism boosting the local economy and providing employment, it has led to environmental issues and increased crime rates. While locals are known for their warmth, instances of tourist harassment have been reported. It's recommended to stay updated with reliable sources before planning a visit.

Current Situation

Hikkaduwa Beach in Sri Lanka is a haven for travelers, offering a mix of adventure and relaxation. Visitors can indulge in water sports like snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing, or swim with sea turtles. Whale watching tours and glass-bottom boat rides offer unique experiences. For relaxation, beach yoga sessions are available, and the local cuisine, especially fresh seafood, is a must-try. The beach also boasts a vibrant nightlife and offers various tours for sightseeing and exploration.

Don't do

In Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka, travelers should avoid dark, deserted areas and secure their belongings. Be cautious of bag snatchers, especially when on the street. Keep money and passport in a body-belt for safety. Avoid crowded public places and large gatherings and . Lastly, use mosquito repellent to protect against dengue fever, especially during the monsoon season

Besttimes

The optimal time to visit Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka, is during the months of January, February, and March, when the weather conditions are most favorable for visitors. If you prefer mildly warm weather, December through February is also a good time. On a daily basis, the best times to visit Hikkaduwa Beach are in the morning and late afternoon. Mornings offer less crowded conditions for activities like surfing and swimming, while late afternoons provide the opportunity to witness beautiful sunsets.

Transpotation

For transportation around Hikkaduwa Beach in Sri Lanka, you have several options. You can enjoy a picturesque train journey from Colombo or use local buses. For more personalized service, consider using the Uber or PickMe apps, which offer a range of vehicles including bikes, three-wheelers, cars, and vans. However, be cautious about using local vehicles as they may charge significantly more than the actual value of the ride.
